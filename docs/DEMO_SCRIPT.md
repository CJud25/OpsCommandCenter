Title: OpsPilot Command Center -- 2-Minute Demo Script

- Cold open (0:00-0:15): "Every operations team knows where work gets stuck. Almost none can prove it, price it, and act on it. OpsPilot does all three -- watch."
- Home (0:15-0:30): open Home. "This is the command center. Two real operations -- a business service desk and a dog rescue -- one question: where are we losing time and money?"
- OpsPilot (0:30-0:50): click OpsPilot, point at the bottleneck headline. "Here is the business side. Stages are ranked by actual delay contribution -- this one stage drives the largest share of total wait. That is the bottleneck, measured, not guessed."
- RescueOps (0:50-1:05): click RescueOps. "Same engine, completely different domain: intake-to-adoption for a rescue. Here the choke point is medical clearance. One system, two operations -- that is the portability test."
- Automation Ranker (1:05-1:20): click Automation Ranker, hover the top candidate. "So what do we fix first? Every candidate is scored on absolute anchors -- volume, delay, error rate -- so a score of 80 means the same thing in both domains. The top of this list is where automation pays back fastest."
- ROI (1:20-1:35): click ROI, open Assumptions, drag the sensitivity slider. "The ROI model is deliberately conservative: labor savings only. Every assumption is exposed here, and the sensitivity slider lets you stress-test it yourself."
- Report Generator (1:35-1:45): click Generate Report. "One click: a stakeholder-ready brief -- findings, ranked opportunities, roadmap. Forward it as-is."
- Micro-automation (1:45-2:00): run the automation, show outbox and audit log. "And it is not just a dashboard. This automation detects missing-document requests, drafts the follow-ups, and logs every action with an idempotency key so it never double-sends."
- Close (2:00-2:15): "Find the bottleneck, price the fix, rank the work, act -- with numbers you can defend. That is OpsPilot."
